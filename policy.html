Payments & Security

We accept cards via Stripe and PayPal. Your card details are never seen or stored by us — payments happen entirely inside Stripe and PayPal's secure systems.

PayPal orders are covered by PayPal Buyer Protection. If something goes wrong, PayPal can step in on your behalf. Stripe payments include built-in fraud detection.

We always prefer to resolve issues directly — email us first. We respond fast and will make things right before any dispute is needed.
